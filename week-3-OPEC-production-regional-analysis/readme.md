# OPEC Oil Production Analysis

## Objectives
1. Total production by region in 1970s
2. Total production by region in 2010s
3. Production per member by region
4. Region comparison

## Queries & Findings

### Query 1: Total production by region in 1970

**Total production**
- Middle East: 1,744,905
- Africa: 652,502
- Latin America: 268,681

### Query 2: Total production by region in 2010

**Total production**
- Middle East: 2,456,911 (↑ 41% growth)
- Africa: 1,763,267 (↑ 170% growth)
- Latin America: 297,887 (↑ 11% growth)

## Africa shows explosive growth

### Query 3: Production per member by region

**Region Production per Member**
- Middle East: 416,001
- Latin America: 147,443
- Africa: 132,605
        
### Query 4: Region comparison

| Region | Production 1970s | Production 2010s | Change | Growth % |
|--------|-----------------|-----------------|--------|----------|
| Middle East | 1,744,905 | 2,456,911 | +712,006 | +40.8% |
| Africa | 455,605 | 673,004 | +217,399 | +47.7% |
| Latin America | 228,891 | 294,987 | +66,076 | +28.8% |

## Key Insights
1. **Middle East** had a steady growth.
2. **Africa** had exploded due to **Angola** growth dominance.
3. **Latin America** had the modest growth.
